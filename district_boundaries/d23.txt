District 23 boundaries are as follows: 
West: Puget Sound including the southern portion of Whidbey Island from the southern tip up to the northern city boundary of Greenbank. 
South: 148th, east to I-5 along I-5 south to I-405 south to where it intersects Locust Way; south to the King/Snohomish County line to Route 2, following Route 2 (including communities on both sides of Route 2) to the King County/Chelan County line
East: The King County/Chelan and Snohomish/Chelan County lines
North: 116th Street in Marysville, from Puget Sound to Cascades
